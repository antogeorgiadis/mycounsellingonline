Simple. Fair. Free to use.

Finding a therapist shouldn’t feel like online shopping.

MyCounselling.Online is a new UK-based therapist directory focused on clarity, transparency and ease of use.

No sponsored profiles, no therapist advertisements, just honest information so you can decide who feels like a good-enough fit for you.

What you’ll find here (once we’re live)

  • No pay-to-rank: therapists aren’t boosted because they pay more.
  • Clear information: fees, reduced rates and availability shown upfront.
  • Plain language: profiles that explain what therapy feels like, not just jargon.
  • Ethical focus: every therapist agrees to simple, transparent principles.

A fair, honest directory focused on helping you find a good fit. No paid boosts, ever.
