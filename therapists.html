For psychotherapists & counsellors

Exploring written therapy together.

We’re a team of qualified, UK-trained psychotherapists building a secure written platform designed for reflective, depth-oriented therapeutic work.

Our aim is to create a space where clients can express themselves in writing, and where therapists can respond with thought, care and clinical integrity, without the pressures of real-time messaging.

If you’re curious about offering written therapy or would like to follow the project as it develops, leave your details below.

The vision (early stage):
Asynchronous / semi-synchronous written sessions Clinically informed design Affordable counselling, without underpaying therapists A thoughtful, paced written exchange, not real-time instant messaging
