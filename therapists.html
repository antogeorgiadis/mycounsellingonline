Looking for a therapist?
Therapist directory built on ethics, not pay-to-rank

A therapist directory that doesn’t sell your place in the list.

mycounselling.online is a UK-based directory being built for therapists who value transparency, accessibility and good-enough ethics over marketing games. No sponsored spots. No pay-to-boost. Just clear, honest profiles.

What will make this directory different?

  • No pay-to-rank: therapists are not ordered by who pays most.
  • Transparent info: fees, sliding scale, and availability upfront.
  • Human-first profiles: real language about what therapy feels like.
  • Ethical commitment: clear, simple principles for everyone listed.

Early intention: to create a calm, non-competitive space where clients can find a good-enough fit and therapists don’t have to shout (or pay more) to be seen.
